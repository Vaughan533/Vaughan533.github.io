Even though boths kittens and things are deeply important, rememebr that HTML tagging is important as well.

The tag h1 will denote the most important heading per container that it exists in.

This means that h1 can be used several times, and will be treated differently each time. As can already be seen, the heading for "Kittens and Things" in the header container and the heading "Kittens" in the article container already look different, even though both are under a h1 tag.
